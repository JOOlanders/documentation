**Events** is a ZOO App focusing on Events.

## Requirements

Events relies on several extensions beside Joomla! and it's minimum version are:

- [Joomla!](http://www.joomla.org/) 3.0.0
- [ZOO](http://yootheme.com/zoo/) 3.3.0
- [ZOOlanders](https://www.zoolanders.com/extensions/zoolanders) 3.3.0
